AI processing

Task text and photos are processed by our AI providers (Anthropic and/or OpenAI) under their API terms, which do not permit training on this data. We do not sell your data. Ever.

Payments

Payments are processed by Apple/Google and subscription state by RevenueCat. We never see your payment details.

Storage & retention

Data is stored in Neon (PostgreSQL) in the United States. Deleting your account in Settings permanently removes your data.
